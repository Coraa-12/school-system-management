How to Apply

We welcome applications from qualified students entering grades 9–12. Follow these simple steps to begin:

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit transcripts from your current school.
  3. Provide two teacher recommendations.
  4. Attend a brief on-campus interview (in person or virtual).

Requirements

  • Application Form (PDF download)
  • Official transcript
  • Standardized test scores (if applicable)
  • Proof of residency
  • Health/immunization records
