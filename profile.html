About Lincoln High School

Home > About Us

Our Heritage

Lincoln High School was established in 1975 with a mission to provide quality education that encourages academic excellence, personal growth, and civic responsibility.

Located in the heart of the city, our campus spans 15 acres with state-of-the-art facilities including science laboratories, computer labs, athletic fields, and a performing arts center.

Our Mission

To inspire and empower students to achieve academic excellence, personal growth, and social responsibility, preparing them to be thoughtful, productive citizens who contribute to a diverse and changing world.

Our Vision

Lincoln High School strives to be a center of educational excellence where students develop the knowledge, skills, and character needed to succeed in a global society while fostering a lifelong love of learning.

Our History

1975

Foundation

Lincoln High School was founded to serve the growing community with just 300 students and 15 teachers.

1990

Expansion

The school expanded with a new science wing and computer labs to accommodate growing enrollment and technological advances.

2005

Arts Center

A state-of-the-art performing arts center was opened, supporting drama, music, and dance programs.

2020

National Recognition

Lincoln High received national recognition for academic excellence and innovative teaching methods.
